Style Guide

Site Logo:

Implementation: The logo to the left may be used in the website as well as for colored printing. The logo to the right may be used for black and white printing. The favicon that is already in place, will be the recognizable icon for PetRescuebyJudy.com.

Typography:

Implementation: We chose the Roboto font because it is a very clean text that is easy to read. Roboto Bold may be used to draw attention to keywords as well as the headings and Roboto Regular may be used as a standard body text. Angel Face Regular was chosen so that we can implement an elegant and decorative text that will also embrace the tone of the website.

Color:

Implementation: The colors are shown with transparent overlays that are 10% lighter and 10% darker for more color choices. This clean and modern palette will bring the website into the 21st century and will draw attention to highlighted elements by the Red tone that we have selected. This is in remembrance to the Red implemented in the old website.

Imagery:

Implementation: Imagery may be used to help communicate and differentitate the different types of animals. It may help engage the viewer into wanting to adopt an animal. It may also help set the mood of the website.
